## Why Competitors Hide Pricing

There are three reasons automation companies do not publish pricing.

### 1. Price Anchoring

If they publish "$5,000 starting" on their website, you know the price before they have a chance to sell you.

If they get you on a call first, they can:
- Understand your pain points
- Quantify the cost of your problem
- Build value before revealing price
- Anchor against your current costs (not their price)

By the time they say "$15,000", you have already mentally calculated that your problem costs $50,000/year. The price seems reasonable.

### 2. Scope Creep

"Automation" is vague. It could mean:
- One workflow connecting two tools
- A full CRM implementation
- Enterprise AI agent deployment
- Custom software development

Without a defined scope, they can quote based on what they think you need (or what you will pay for), not what you actually need.

### 3. Negotiation Room

If pricing is published, there is no room to negotiate.

If pricing is custom, they can:
- Start high ($25,000)
- Come down if you push back ($15,000)
- Make you feel like you got a deal
- Still charge 3x what a transparent company would

## What They Actually Charge

Based on our research (website analysis, industry reports, and client conversations who have worked with these firms), here is the typical pricing:

### Entry-Level Projects
- **Price:** $1,000-$5,000
- **Scope:** Single workflow, basic CRM setup
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks
- **Ongoing:** $500-$2,000/month support retainer

### Mid-Market Projects
- **Price:** $5,000-$15,000
- **Scope:** Multi-workflow, dashboard, team training
- **Timeline:** 4-8 weeks
- **Ongoing:** $1,000-$3,000/month support retainer

### Enterprise Projects
- **Price:** $15,000-$50,000+
- **Scope:** Full automation suite, custom integrations, AI agents
- **Timeline:** 8-16 weeks
- **Ongoing:** $3,000-$10,000/month support retainer

### The Hidden Cost: Monthly Retainers

Most competitors charge ongoing monthly fees:
- **Support retainer:** $500-$5,000/month
- **Hosting fees:** $100-$500/month
- **Maintenance:** $500-$2,000/month
- **"Account management":** $500-$3,000/month

Over 5 years, a $15,000 project with $2,000/month retainer becomes:
- **Year 1:** $15,000 + $24,000 = $39,000
- **Years 2-5:** $24,000 × 4 = $96,000
- **5-year total:** $135,000
